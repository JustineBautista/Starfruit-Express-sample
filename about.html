Our story

We saw how much energy restaurants were losing to scattered systems.

What should have been about food, guests, and growth had become a daily battle with rising costs, fragmented ordering, and stress behind the scenes. We built Starfruit to change that — by taking on the logistics so you don't have to.

When restaurants feel supported again, the whole room changes. We exist to make that feeling real — order by order, kitchen by kitchen.
